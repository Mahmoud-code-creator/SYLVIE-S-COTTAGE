About the cottage

Light-filled interiors, connected to nature.

Large glass doors open directly onto the wooden deck, bringing the forest indoors. The living room, bedroom and kitchen have been carefully styled with soft tones, natural wood and warm lighting.

Wake up to trees outside your window, enjoy coffee on the terrace and end the day in the outdoor jacuzzi overlooking the valley. SYLVIE’S COTTAGE is designed for guests who love calm, design and a touch of luxury in nature.
